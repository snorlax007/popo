How OTA Works
  1. Upload a .bin file and mark it stable
  2. Go to a device → push OTA notification via MQTT
  3. Device downloads and verifies SHA256
  4. Device flashes and reboots on next idle

Only stable releases are served to devices via GET /api/ota/check. Unstable builds can be tested by direct URL.
